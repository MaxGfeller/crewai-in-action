# Managing Chickens

The Chickens page is where you manage all your chicken records. You can add new chickens, edit existing ones, and delete chickens from your flock.

![Chickens Page](/images/chickens.png)

## Viewing Your Flock

The chickens page displays all your chickens in a table format with the following columns:

- **Name** - The name of the chicken
- **Breed** - The breed of the chicken (if specified)
- **Age** - Age in months
- **Weight** - Weight in kilograms
- **Status** - Current status (active, retired, or sick)
- **Notes** - Additional notes about the chicken
- **Actions** - Edit and Delete buttons

## Adding a New Chicken

To add a new chicken to your flock:

1. Click the **"Add Chicken"** button at the top right of the page
2. Fill in the chicken details in the dialog that appears:
   - **Name** (required) - Enter the chicken's name
   - **Breed** (optional) - Specify the breed
   - **Age (months)** (optional) - Enter the age in months
   - **Weight (kg)** (optional) - Enter the weight in kilograms
   - **Status** - Select from Active, Retired, or Sick
   - **Notes** (optional) - Add any additional notes
3. Click **"Create"** to save the new chicken

![Add Chicken Dialog](/images/add-chicken.png)

<Info>
  Only the Name field is required. All other fields are optional and can be filled in later.
</Info>

## Editing a Chicken

To update an existing chicken's information:

1. Find the chicken in the table
2. Click the **"Edit"** button in the Actions column
3. Modify any fields in the edit dialog
4. Click **"Update"** to save your changes

The edit dialog is pre-filled with the current chicken information, making it easy to make quick updates.

## Deleting a Chicken

To remove a chicken from your flock:

1. Find the chicken in the table
2. Click the **"Delete"** button in the Actions column
3. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to remove the chicken before confirming.
</Warning>

## Chicken Status

You can set three different statuses for your chickens:

- **Active** - The chicken is currently active and healthy
- **Retired** - The chicken has been retired (e.g., no longer laying eggs)
- **Sick** - The chicken is currently sick and may need attention

Status is displayed with color-coded badges:
- Active: Green badge
- Sick: Red badge
- Retired: Gray badge

## Best Practices

- **Keep records updated** - Regularly update chicken information as they grow or their status changes
- **Add detailed notes** - Use the notes field to track important information like health issues, behavior, or special care requirements
- **Update status promptly** - Change status when chickens become sick or are retired to keep accurate records
- **Record breed information** - Adding breed information helps with the dashboard statistics and flock management
