# Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your flock with key statistics and visualizations.

![Dashboard](/images/dashboard.png)

## Key Metrics

The dashboard displays three main statistics cards:

### Total Chickens
Shows the total number of chickens in your flock, regardless of status.

### Active Chickens
Displays the count of chickens currently marked as "active" status.

### Total Weight
Shows the combined weight of all chickens in your flock in kilograms.

## Distribution Charts

### Chickens by Breed
This section shows how your flock is distributed across different breeds. Each breed is listed with its count, helping you understand the diversity of your flock.

<Warning>
  If you haven't added breed information to your chickens, this section will show "No breed data available".
</Warning>

### Chickens by Status
This chart displays the distribution of chickens by their current status:
- **Active** - Chickens that are currently active
- **Retired** - Chickens that have been retired
- **Sick** - Chickens marked as sick

## Using the Dashboard

The dashboard automatically updates whenever you:
- Add new chickens
- Update chicken information
- Change chicken status
- Delete chickens

All statistics are calculated in real-time based on your current flock data.

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can:
- Navigate to **Chickens** page to manage your flock
- View detailed statistics about your chickens
- Monitor the health and status of your flock at a glance
