# Getting Started

Welcome to Chicken CRM! This guide will help you log in and start managing your flock.

## Logging In

To access Chicken CRM, navigate to the login page. You'll see a clean login form where you can enter your credentials.

<Info>
  **Demo Account**: Use `demo@example.com` with password `password123` to explore the application.
</Info>

### Login Steps

1. **Enter your email address** in the Email field
2. **Enter your password** in the Password field
3. **Click the Login button** to authenticate

![Login Page](/images/login.png)

After successful login, you'll be redirected to your Dashboard where you can see an overview of your flock statistics.

## Creating an Account

If you don't have an account yet:

1. Click the **"Sign up"** link on the login page
2. Fill in your name, email, and password
3. Submit the form to create your account
4. You'll be automatically logged in after registration

## Navigation

Once logged in, you'll see a navigation bar at the top with:

- **🐔 Chicken CRM** - Logo that links to the dashboard
- **Dashboard** - View statistics and overview
- **Chickens** - Manage your chicken records
- **Logout** - Sign out of your account
