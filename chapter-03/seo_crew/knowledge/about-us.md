# Storique

Storique is a Swiss company that operates a platform where customers can create uniquely personal story books with the help of AI. For that, they define what characters should be in their story and upload a few photos of them, then choose a suggested personalized story (or write one themselves). After ordering, a team of AI agents then writes and illustrates the book, with images that look like they were hand-drawn but where the characters are still distinctively recognizable.

Each book produced with Storique is unique, and all stories are created specifically for those characters. If you can imagine a book, Storique can help you write it!
